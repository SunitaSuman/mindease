Breathing Exercises

Breathing exercises can help reduce anxiety and stress. Try these techniques:

4-7-8 Breathing

Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 7 seconds, exhale for 8 seconds. Repeat 4-6 times.

Box Breathing

Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 4 seconds, exhale for 4 seconds, hold for 4 seconds. Repeat.

Diaphragmatic Breathing

Breathe deeply into your diaphragm (belly), not your chest. Place a hand on your belly to feel it rise and fall.
